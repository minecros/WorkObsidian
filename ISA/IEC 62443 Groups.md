# General 
The General group sets the foundational knowledge required for understanding and applying the rest of the standard.
- Primary Focus: Foundational concepts and terminology.
- Intended Audience: All stakeholders (asset owners, product suppliers, system integrators, and policymakers).![[Pasted image 20260427135220.png]]
# Policies and Procedures
 The Policies & Procedures group outlines the policies and best practices organizations should follow to manage cybersecurity risk effectively.
- Primary Focus: Security program requirements for organizations.
- Intended Audience: Asset owners, service providers, IT/security managers.

# System
The System group focuses on securing systems, particularly the integration of components and overall system-level risk.
- Primary Focus: Security requirements and processes for systems.
- Intended Audience: System integrators, designers, and cybersecurity architects.
![[Pasted image 20260427135401.png]]
# Component
The **Component** group ensures that components are **built with security in mind** from the start, aligning with **secure-by-design** principles.
- **Primary Focus:** Requirements for individual IACS components (e.g., PLCs, HMIs).
- **Intended Audience:** Product developers, equipment manufacturers, and vendors. ![[Pasted image 20260427135451.png]]
# Profiles
The **Profiles** group is new. It helps **bridge the gap** between high-level security requirements and **practical implementation** in specific environments.
**Profiles** are essentially **customized implementations** or **interpretations** of the 62443 requirements tailored to specific use cases, industries, or asset types.
They will make the standard more **actionable**, **relevant**, and **easier to implement**.
- **Primary Focus:** Customize 62443 requirements to specific environments or use cases.
- **Intended Audience:** All stakeholders, especially asset owners and integrators.
![[Pasted image 20260427135532.png]]
# Evaluation
The **Evaluation** group aims to provide **methodologies** and **criteria** for **assessing and certifying the security of IACS components, systems, and processes.**
- **Primary Focus**: Ensure consistency, reliability, and credibility in how conformance to 62443 is verified.
- **Audience:** All stakeholders, including certification bodies, regulators, and industry groups.
